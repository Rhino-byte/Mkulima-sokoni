How Mkulima sokoni Works

From sign-up to your first sale or purchase — here's how the platform works for every user.

🚀 Getting Started

Three simple steps to join Kenya's fairest agricultural marketplace.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or Google account. It takes less than a minute. Choose your role: Farmer, Buyer, or Agro-Dealer.

2

Complete Your Profile

Add your farm name, location, contact details, and a profile photo. A complete profile builds trust and helps buyers find you.

3

Start Trading

Farmers: list your produce with photos and pricing. Buyers: browse the marketplace and contact sellers directly. It's that simple.

👥 For Each Role

Mkulima sokoni is built for three types of users, each with tailored features.

🌾

Farmers

  • List unlimited products with photos
  • Set your own prices — no middlemen
  • Receive direct inquiries from buyers
  • Track product views and interest
  • Manage orders from your dashboard
  • Get verified for more buyer trust
🛒

Buyers

  • Browse produce from all 47 counties
  • Filter by category, location, and price
  • Contact farmers directly via WhatsApp or call
  • Create tenders for bulk purchases
  • Track your order history
  • View verified seller profiles
🏪

Agro-Dealers

  • List farming supplies and agro-products
  • Reach farmers looking for inputs
  • Manage your inventory digitally
  • Process orders through your dashboard
  • Build your brand with a public profile
  • Connect with the farming community

🔒 Trust & Safety

How we keep the marketplace clean and fair.

✓

User Verification

Our admin team reviews submitted documents (ID, farm registration, business license) and grants verified badges. Verified users are trusted more by the community.

✓

Real Listings Only

We actively monitor and remove fake, misleading, or inappropriate listings. Every product must be a real agricultural good with accurate pricing.

✓

Direct Communication

Buyers and sellers communicate directly — no hidden fees or intermediaries. You negotiate your own terms and arrange your own delivery.
