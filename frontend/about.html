About Mkulima sokoni

Empowering Kenyan farmers and buyers through a fair, transparent digital marketplace.

🌱 Our Story

Mkulima sokoni was born from a simple observation: hardworking Kenyan farmers deserve fair prices for their produce, and buyers deserve fresh, quality products straight from the source.

Too often, middlemen capture the value that should go to the people who grow our food. Mkulima sokoni — meaning "Clean Market" in Swahili — was created to eliminate that gap. We connect farmers directly with buyers, agro-dealers, and communities across all 47 counties in Kenya.

What started as a vision to make agricultural trade fairer has grown into a platform trusted by farmers and buyers alike, where every transaction is transparent and every price is fair.

🎯 Mission & Vision

🚀

Our Mission

To create Africa's most trusted agricultural marketplace that empowers smallholder farmers with fair prices, direct market access, and the tools they need to thrive.

🔭

A Kenya where every farmer — from Turkana to Kwale — can sell their produce at a fair price, and every buyer can access fresh, quality agricultural products with confidence.
